Who are we!

NXT Museum is the first museum in the Netherlands dedicated to new media art. We focus on art that uses modern tools to embody contemporary times. We believe that the tools used in artistic expression reflect the era we live in. That makes them the perfect means to understand contemporary complexity, allowing us to recognize our reality.

History

NXT Museum, founded by the young entrepreneur Merel van Helsdingen, exhibits large-scale art installations, developed through interdisciplinary collaborations between established and emerging artists, designers, technicians, musicians, and scientists.

Our Goal

It is our mission to enable new and next iterations of creative expression. Everything we do, desire, and fear directly relates to the future. It is one of the most fascinating concepts for the human mind. An exciting source of inspiration that raises endless questions. Nowadays, these questions are more important and complex than ever. What's next? We don't know, but we are endlessly curious to explore together.
